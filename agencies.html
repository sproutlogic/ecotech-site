Campaign Workflow

From brief to live in
Instant - post approval.

1
Day 0
Briefing & Inventory Selection
Share your client brief — geography, audience profile, campaign objective, flight dates. We return a curated inventory recommendation with venue-level dwell data and audience composition within the same business day.
2
Day 0–1
IO & Creative Submission
Standard Insertion Order accepted — same format your team already uses. Creative assets uploaded via our agency portal or emailed directly to your account lead. Full spec sheet provided at onboarding.
3
Day 1–2
Creative Review & Approval
Our team reviews for technical compliance (resolution, format, safe zones). You receive confirmation or revision notes within 4 business hours. No drawn-out approval loops — a single clear checklist.
4
Day 2
Campaign Goes Live
Approved creative is pushed to the network. IoT sensors activate. Real-time dwell data starts recording. You receive a go-live confirmation with venue IDs and sensor activation timestamps for your records.
5
During Flight
Live Dashboard Access
Agency dashboard provides real-time dwell events, daily impression tallies, and attention score by venue. Exportable CSV at any time. Share live links with your client for on-demand transparency — a first for Indian OOH.
6
Post Campaign
Proof of Performance Report
Full post-campaign report delivered within 3 business days of flight end. Includes: total verified dwell events, time-of-day distribution, peak attention windows, and co-branded ESG impact summary ready for client presentations and sustainability disclosures.
